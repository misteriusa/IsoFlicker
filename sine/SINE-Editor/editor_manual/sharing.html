Sharing

On the application's website, we've created a Preset sharing platform, where users can share their work and download other user's Presets.
The sharing platform is totally open: anyone can use it without the need of a registration.

Test your Preset!

Before you share your Preset, you should test it to make sure everything works as intended: please do not upload buggy or pointless Presets.

Sharing

First, save your Preset. The file name will be visible, so give it a meaningful and short name, for instance: lucid.sin for Lucid dream is fine, but Preset.sin isn't.
Now, open the file menu and click share: your browser will open on the sharing platform:
Now, click browse, select the file you want to upload and hit Upload.
The Preset will be checked to see if it's valid, and if everything's fine, it will be shown immediately, otherwise an error will tell you what was wrong with it.
Presets shared on this platform must have a title, an author and a description.
As you can see, users can see the description, vote your Preset, share it, and leave comments.

Abuse and license

We check everything that's uploaded by the users: broken Presets, fake Presets, plagiarized Presets and spam will be removed immediately.
We DO NOT collect personal data of any kind through the sharing platform.
All the Presets uploaded are distributed under the Creative Commons Attribution 3.0 Unported license, unless otherwise specified in the Preset description.
